Order without the wait.

Jijiko is a QR-code ordering system built for cafés and small restaurants in Dar es Salaam — scan the code on your table, order, and watch it come to you. No app to download, no flagging down a waiter.

How it works

Three steps, no queueing

Built around how people actually behave at a table — phone in hand, in no mood to wait for a waiter to be free.

01

Scan the table

Every table has its own QR code. Scan it and the menu opens straight to that table — no login required.

02

Order and customize

Pick items, adjust sugar, size, or add notes. Order solo or open the table to friends ordering together.

03

Track it live

Watch the order move from kitchen to your table in real time, and pay by cash or in-app when it arrives.

Behind the counter

Everyone on staff gets their own view

Not just a customer menu — a full flow from kitchen to till.

Kitchen

Cooks see incoming orders the moment they're placed and mark them ready without touching paper tickets.

Floor staff

Attendants see what's ready to deliver, handle cash payments, and respond to table assistance calls.

Owner

One dashboard for the menu, staff, sales, and daily reports — from a phone or a laptop.

Built for here, not adapted from somewhere else
